Artificial Psychosis Project Overview

Overview

SENTAX Crew: Artificial Psychosis is a psychological horror experience focused on identity breakdown, neural manipulation and distorted reality.

The player is placed inside a restricted mountain facility where experiments on human cognition and artificial intelligence are conducted in secrecy.

Gameplay Concept

• First-person psychological horror
• Memory distortion mechanics
• Hallucination-based environments
• AI-driven behavioral shifts
• Non-linear narrative progression

Story Layer

Subjects begin to lose their sense of identity as artificial signals interfere with perception. Religious hallucinations, voices and environmental changes become indistinguishable from reality.

The deeper the subject goes, the less human they remain.
