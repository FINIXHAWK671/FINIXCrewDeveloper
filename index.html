About

FINIXCREW Developer is a student-based team focused on immersive horror experiences using Godot Engine. This is a volunteer-driven project built through passion, learning, and collaboration.

SENTAX Crew: Artificial Psychosis

In collaboration with Indonesia developers, SENTAX Crew: Artificial Psychosis is a psychological horror project set in a restricted research facility isolated from society.


The facility conducts experimental procedures on human and animal subjects involving neural manipulation, artificial intelligence integration, and behavioral conditioning.


SSRA (Synaptic Simulation Research Authority) operates the facility, aiming to merge biological consciousness with adaptive AI systems.


As experiments progress, subjects begin experiencing distorted reality, religious hallucinations, and loss of identity, blurring the boundary between faith, control, and madness.
