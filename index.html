About

FINIXCREW DEVELOPER is a student-based horror development team focused on immersive psychological experiences using Godot Engine. This is a volunteer-driven project created through passion, learning and collaboration.

SENTAX Crew: Artificial Psychosis

Developed in collaboration with international developers across Indonesia, Malaysia, Russia, United States, Portugal, Spain and other regions, this project takes place in a remote mountain facility located in a classified [REDACTED] region.


The facility is operated by SSRA (Synaptic Simulation Research Authority), conducting experimental procedures on human and animal subjects involving neural manipulation, artificial intelligence integration and behavioral conditioning.


As experiments progress, subjects begin experiencing distorted perception, religious hallucinations and loss of identity, blurring the line between reality, faith and psychological control.
